Form

Enter the route information

You must enter the information of the starting point, arrival point and the distance value in string format all together and separated by commas each route example: AB5, BC4, CD8, DC8, DE6, AD5, CE2, EB3, AE7

The distance of the route

The point of entry, arrival and stops must be entered for example A, B, C

The number of trips from a point of departure to a point of arrival with a maximum number of stops.

For example C-C and 3

The number of trips from a point of departure to a point of arrival with an exact number of stops.

For example A-C and 4

The length of the shortest route (in terms of distance to travel)

For example A-C

The number of different routes from a starting point and an arrival point with a distance less than a number x

For example C-C and 30
